Global Scientific Projects

Discover ongoing scientific projects that are pushing the boundaries of knowledge across various fields:

ATLAS Experiment (CERN)

A particle physics experiment at the Large Hadron Collider, exploring fundamental forces and particles in the universe.

Human Brain Project

A European project aiming to understand the human brain and develop new technologies in neuroscience and artificial intelligence.

ITER Fusion Project

An international nuclear fusion research and engineering project to prove the feasibility of fusion as a large-scale energy source.

Square Kilometre Array (SKA)

A global radio telescope initiative to observe the universe and study cosmic phenomena such as black holes and the origins of galaxies.
